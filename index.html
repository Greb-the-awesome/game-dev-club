How to Play

This is a clicker game where you try to get as many ECs as possible by clicking the big button.

You also have the orzosity property which is how many ECs you gain on a single click, and the aura property, which is the rate of passive EC generation.

Upgrades increase your orzosity/aura:

  • Crimson Education increases your orzosity.
  • DECA also increases your orzosity.
  • Glazebaiting increases your aura.

Lastly there is admitting which resets all your progress, but gives you multipliers!
