Available Techniques
Flop Wrist An offensive move that puts pressure on your opponent’s wrist, effective for destabilizing them.

King’s Move A defensive technique that allows you to maintain resistance at a longer range, effective for countering aggressive moves.
Back Pressure Uses backward force to resist your opponent’s pull, helping you stay in control.
Dragging Moves your opponent’s hand to your side, capitalizing on their lack of control.
Press A finishing move that relies on body weight and downward pressure to quickly end the match.
